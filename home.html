Software Developer based on Long Island

I am a recent graduate of Roger Williams University. I graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science with a specialization in Data Science, and a minor in Mathematics. At graduation I was awarded the Top Computer Science Student Award for 2022. I was a member of the Honor's Program at RWU.


During my senior year, I was a part of the development team for DRIVE, an Android application for giving and receiving virtual tours over video calls. The finished product went beyond the expectations given to our team, with the final build of the application having extra features implemented than initially required. One of the key factors to this project's success was my ability to learn new technologies and quickly integrate them into the application. Teamwork was also another key factor, with our team working closely together using an Agile approach, both in-person and through web calls. Working on this app gave me experience working with the React.js framework, as well as Javascript, Socket.io WebSockets, and Node.js.


I am currently seeking a remote position in web or mobile development; frontend, backend, or full-stack.
